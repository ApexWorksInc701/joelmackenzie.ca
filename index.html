Podcasting

Joel is the creator, a creative director, and a host of the ArtsNW podcast. It was made with the New West Arts Council to promote and support artists in the city, as well as tell compelling narratives about the lives of modern artists.
Listen to it on the Arts Council's website, on Apple Podcasts, on Spotify, or anywhere you download podcasts.
